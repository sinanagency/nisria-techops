# Ad Grants — Starter Campaigns, Keywords & Ad Copy (ready to paste)

Drop-in starter for the 5-campaign structure in `google-ad-grants-playbook.md`. Edit ⚑ placeholders (real outcomes, URLs, brand specifics) before launch. All ads are Responsive Search Ads (RSAs): 15 headlines / 4 descriptions max; provide strong ones, Google mixes.

> Rules baked in: ≥2 ad groups/campaign, ≥2 RSAs/group, no single-word/generic keywords, ≥2 sitelinks, geo-targeted, conversion tracking on. Keep CTR ≥5%.
