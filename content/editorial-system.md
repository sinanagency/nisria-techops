# Content & Publishing Engine (Pillar 1)

Runs daily social across 3 brands, weekly blogs (Nisria + Maisha), and a weekly Nisria newsletter — mostly Claude-drafted, human-reviewed, on a fixed cadence so it never depends on inspiration.

> Tools: Squarespace (sites + blogs), Canva (graphics), Claude (drafting), Givebutter/Substack (newsletter), Meta Business Suite (FB/IG scheduling). Owner: Delegate + AI. Nur owns cause-marketing direction only.

## Social cadence matrix

| Brand | Platforms | Daily posts | Weekly anchor |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Nisria** | IG, FB, LinkedIn, YouTube, TikTok, Pinterest (Reddit later) | 1/day rotating | 1 blog + newsletter |
| **Maisha** | IG, FB, LinkedIn, TikTok, Pinterest | 1/day rotating | 1 blog |
| **AHADI** | IG, FB, Pinterest, TikTok | 1/day (medium priority) | — |

Reuse smart: 1 "hero" piece/week per brand → atomized into 5–7 platform-native posts (Reels/short-video, carousel, quote card, story, Pin, LinkedIn text). Don't create 7 separate ideas; spin 1 into 7.

## Content pillars (the recurring buckets — never stare at a blank page)

1. **Impact** — a beneficiary story / outcome (consent-gated, from Supabase profiles).
2. **Behind-the-scenes** — Kenya team, field visits, process.
3. **Education** — the cause, the need, the "why" (myth-busting, stats).
4. **Gratitude / donor spotlight** — thank donors, show where money goes.
5. **Call-to-action** — donate, volunteer, share, shop (The Folklore).
6. **Brand/mission** — values, vision, milestones.

Weekly rhythm = rotate pillars Mon–Sun so each brand hits all six across the week.

## Weekly production workflow

```
MON  Plan: fill editorial calendar for the week (pillars × brands × platforms)
MON  Draft blogs (Claude) for Nisria + Maisha → _DRAFT in Drive 07_CONTENT/Blog Drafts
TUE  Review/edit blogs (human) → publish to Squarespace
TUE  Draft week's social (Claude) from the 2 hero pieces → captions + hashtags
WED  Design (Canva) graphics/carousels from templates
WED  Schedule social (Meta Business Suite for FB/IG; native/3rd-party for others)
THU  Draft Nisria newsletter (Claude) from week's best content → Givebutter/Substack
FRI  Review + send newsletter
ONGOING  Engage/respond (community management)
```

## Blog pipeline (Claude → Squarespace)

- Prompt seed per post: topic + pillar + 1 source fact + brand voice file → 600–900 word draft, H2 structure, meta description, suggested image.
- Human pass: fact-check (esp. beneficiary detail + consent), localize, add real photos.
- Publish to Squarespace; cross-post excerpt to LinkedIn + newsletter.

## Newsletter (weekly, Nisria)

- Platform: **Givebutter** (keeps donors + email in one place; native to fundraising) — Substack as alt for public/SEO reach. ⚑ confirm with Nur which primary.
- Structure: 1 impact story · 1 ask (campaign/donate) · 1 update · 1 way-to-help (share/shop).
- Built from the week's best blog + social, assembled by Claude, reviewed before send.

## Reddit (deferred)

Useful eventually (cause subreddits, AMAs, awareness) but high-effort and punishes promotion. **Not now** — revisit after the core channels are humming. Listed as a future Nisria channel only.
