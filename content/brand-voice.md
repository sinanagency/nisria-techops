# Brand Voice & Tone (prelim — ⚑ refine with Nur)

Shared north star, then per-brand nuance. Used as the system prompt seed for all Claude-drafted content so three brands stay distinct but coherent.

## Shared principles (all three brands)

- **Dignified, never pitiful.** Beneficiaries are people with agency, not props. No poverty-porn, no guilt-bait.
- **Specific over sweeping.** "Aisha now walks 10 minutes to school instead of two hours" beats "we change lives."
- **Warm, plain, editorial.** Short sentences. Real words. No NGO jargon ("synergies", "capacity-building" only when literally meant).
- **Show the money's path.** Donors trust transparency: what came in, what it did.
- **No clichés / no hype.** Avoid "game-changing", "passionate about", "make a difference" as filler.

## Do / Don't

**Do:** active voice · name real outcomes · credit the Kenya team · invite (not pressure) · localize to the audience · pair every ask with proof.
**Don't:** stock-photo a "sad child" · imply donors are saviors · over-claim numbers · use a beneficiary's image/story without consent (see `data/beneficiary-intake.md`) · post the same caption across platforms verbatim.
