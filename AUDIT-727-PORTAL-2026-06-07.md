### Misplacements found
1. **Data entry forms placed after information** - The most important user actions (logging receipts, adding payments) are placed at the bottom of the page, requiring multiple scrolls to access.
2. **Paid history collapsed by default** - Critical historical context is hidden behind a disclosure triangle, making it harder to find past transactions.
3. **Reminders and Salaries in main view** - These are primarily for planning, not immediate action, but they're given prominent space in the main view instead of being tucked away in a more appropriate section.
4. **Key financial summary in the middle** - Treasury summary is sandwiched between information and tools, making it less discoverable.

## C. Broader structural findings
### Finding 1: Task card stacking violates NISRIA-DESIGN-SYSTEM.md Section 4 canonical components
- File: `app/tasks/page.tsx:71-117`
- Problem: Using raw div containers instead of proper card components as defined in NISRIA-DESIGN-SYSTEM.md
- Proposed: Replace raw divs with `.card` class and proper component structure from Section 4
- Severity: P1

### Finding 2: Inconsistent information hierarchy in Finance view
- File: `app/finance/page.tsx:337-797`
- Problem: Mix of "information" and "tools" sections without clear visual hierarchy. Key tools are buried in the middle of information.
- Proposed: Reorganize using clear visual separation and placement based on user intent (information at top, actions at bottom)
- Severity: P1

### Finding 3: Excessive click depth for core workflows
- File: `app/tasks/page.tsx:71-117`, `app/finance/page.tsx:337-797`
- Problem: Tasks require multiple clicks to complete, and Finance requires scrolling through extensive content to reach key actions
- Proposed: Implement keyboard shortcuts, quick action bars, and reduce nesting levels
- Severity: P1

### Finding 4: Missing loading states and empty states in Finance
- File: `app/finance/page.tsx:248-290`, `app/finance/page.tsx:337-337`
- Problem: No loading indicators for initial data fetch or empty states for all collapsible sections
- Proposed: Add skeleton loaders and empty state components as per NISRIA-DESIGN-SYSTEM.md Section 4
- Severity: P2

### Finding 5: Duplicate functionality in Finance view
- File: `app/finance/page.tsx:451-514`, `app/finance/page.tsx:341-449`
- Problem: Salaries and Reminders sections both handle similar "due soon" functionality
- Proposed: Consolidate similar functionality into one unified "Obligations" section with clear categorization
- Severity: P2
