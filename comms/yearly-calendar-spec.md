# Yearly Calendar — Nur & Staff (Pillar 3)

A shared annual calendar so campaigns, content, reporting, and grant deadlines are visible and never collide. Tools: Google Calendar (shared) + Zoom. Owner: Nur (annual setup + monthly review).

## Calendars (layered, shared)

| Calendar | Holds | Color |
|---|---|---|
| **Fundraising** | seasonal campaigns (4–6/yr), giving days (#GivingTuesday, year-end, Ramadan), grant deadlines | red |
| **Content** | blog days, newsletter sends, social themes, shoots | blue |
| **Operations** | team calls, monthly reviews, reporting deadlines, board/governance | green |
| **Programs (Kenya)** | field visits, intakes, distributions, program milestones | orange |

## Annual anchors to pre-load (⚑ confirm dates/relevance with Nur)

- **Ramadan / Eid** appeal window (Maisha/Nisria audiences) — major giving moment.
- **Back-to-school** (Kenya term dates) — education program + appeal.
- **#GivingTuesday** (late Nov) — global giving day.
- **Year-end giving** (Dec) — biggest secular giving window.
- **World days** relevant to the cause (e.g. children/education days) — content hooks.
- **Quarterly**: impact report due, donor report due, financial review, data-hygiene review.
- **Annual**: report, audit, strategy/calendar planning.

## Rules

- **Campaigns get a runway**: each seasonal campaign = planning block (-4 wks) → live → wrap/thank (+1 wk) on the calendar, not just the day-of.
- **No collisions**: don't launch two big asks the same week; space appeals.
- **Reporting deadlines** (grants, board) blocked well ahead with prep reminders.
- **Monthly review** (Nur): look 6–8 weeks ahead, confirm prep is underway.

## Setup steps

1. Create the 4 shared Google Calendars under the Workspace; share with Nur + leads (+ relevant Kenya team).
2. Pre-load annual anchors + known deadlines.
3. Link campaign entries to their Givebutter campaign + Drive folder.
4. Set prep reminders (e.g. campaign planning fires 4 weeks before live).
5. Zoom links on recurring team calls / monthly reviews.

## Automation candidate

Grant deadlines from Supabase `grant_applications` → auto-create calendar events + prep reminders; campaign dates from `campaigns` → calendar. See `automation/automation-map.md`.
